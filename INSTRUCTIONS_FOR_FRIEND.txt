How to Install CullSnap on Mac

1. Download and unzip CullSnap.app.zip.

2. Drag the CullSnap app into your Applications folder.

IMPORTANT: Do not double-click the app yet.

3. Right-Click (or Control+Click) the app icon.

4. Select Open from the menu.

5. A popup will appear saying "Cannot verify developer". Click the "Open" button in that popup.

The app will launch. You only need to do this "Right-Click" trick the first time.
